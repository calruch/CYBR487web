# AI Usage

This repository allows AI-assisted work under the following rules.

## Allowed

- AI may be used for **documentation drafting** (with human proofreading for correctness).
- AI may be used for **planning** (e.g., breaking work into issues/tasks).
- AI may be used for **single lines / small snippets** that solve a specific task.
- AI may be used for **troubleshooting** after standard debugging steps are attempted.

## Not allowed

- Copy/pasting large blocks of generated code without review.
- Generating “final answers” that are not verified against the actual codebase.

## Disclosure

When AI is used in a meaningful way, summarize what it was used for in the relevant PR/commit message.
